About Us

Arun & Co. is a sole proprietorship owned company, incorporated in the year 1986. The company has started business as a sole proprietorship owned firm as well as engaged in manufacturing, trading, wholesaling, and supplying a wide assortment of Dowel Pin, HSFG Washer, T Handle Allen Key Wrench, Metal Bolt, and Spring Washer. Constant improvement and innovation of products forms the core of our firm and make us the most favored choice of maximum clientele.
